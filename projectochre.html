About Ochre

So, you are an alien on large research vessel monitoring a primitive world. Watching as they grow. You are a bit of a failure. You failed your quantum physics test for a third time, and supervision has decided that you should just be recycled.

You find out about this and quickly make your escape. You take an escape pod down to the surface without any equipment. You awake from the impact to a thumping noise. Goblins are checking out your escape pod that fell from the heavens. Wait... Are they worshipping you?

Yep, alien lands on a fantasy D&D style world and has to build a civilization that can produce spacecraft to escape.

Powered by a GOAP based AI system, you guide a colony into a thriving civilization. Each character in the world has their own wants and desires and works to fulfil them. You can't control any of them directly, but you can set work orders and pay people for their work. A 3D voxel engine is already built for this project, and it all happens in first person.
